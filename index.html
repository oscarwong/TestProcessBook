introduction

This process book details and guides through our group's design process. Our assignment was to identify a problem that was prevalent among college students and to design a solution for it. After many thorough interviews and dialogues with colleagues, we discovered that our classmates were very busy and had multiple obligations to juggle everyday. Because of this, students had disrupted eating schedules and established unhealthy eating habits. We decided to tackle this issue and find a solution that would be compatible with our users.

This website is dedicated towards summarizing the different stages that took place during the project. This includes, but is not exclusive to, our team dynamics, responsibilities, and goals.
